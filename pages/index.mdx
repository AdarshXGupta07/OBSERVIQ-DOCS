## Why ObservIQ?

Every company is spending money on AI APIs but flying completely blind in production. When your AI starts hallucinating, giving wrong answers, or burning through your budget — you have no idea.

ObservIQ is the safety net. **Two lines of Python code**, and you instantly get full visibility into every AI call your app makes.

```python
from observiq_sdk import ObservIQ

oiq = ObservIQ(api_key="oiq_...")
response = oiq.trace(groq.chat.completions.create)(
    model="llama-3.3-70b-versatile",
    messages=[{"role": "user", "content": "Hello!"}]
)
```

That's it. Every call is now tracked.
